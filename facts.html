Penguin History

60 Million Years in the Making

🦕
~66 Million Years Ago
The non-avian dinosaurs go extinct. The common ancestor of penguins and other modern birds begins to diversify rapidly.
🦅
~60 Million Years Ago
The earliest known penguin ancestor, Waimanu manneringi, appears in what is now New Zealand. It was already flightless and aquatic.
🦴
~37 Million Years Ago
Fossil evidence shows giant penguins over 6 feet tall roaming ancient seas — twice the size of Emperor penguins today.
🗺️
1520 CE
Explorer Ferdinand Magellan becomes the first European to document penguins, encountering Magellanic penguins during his circumnavigation of the globe.
📅
Today
18 recognized species remain, with 10 classified as Vulnerable, Endangered, or Near Threatened due to climate change, overfishing, and pollution.
